About Farm Fresh

Empowering farmers with tools and knowledge since 2026

Our Story

Farm Fresh was born from a simple observation: small-scale farmers in Kenya struggle to access quality farming tools and reliable plant care information. We saw farmers wasting time searching for equipment, losing crops to preventable diseases, and missing out on market opportunities due to lack of information.

Our platform connects farmers with tool suppliers, provides expert growing guides, and creates a community where farmers can share knowledge. We believe that with the right tools and information, every farmer can achieve a bountiful harvest.

Our Mission

🌱 Accessible Tools

Make farming equipment accessible and affordable to every farmer through our rental directory.

📚 Knowledge Sharing

Provide expert, easy-to-understand plant care guides in local languages and contexts.

🤝 Community

Build a supportive community where farmers can learn from each other's experiences.

🌍 Sustainability

Promote sustainable farming practices that protect the environment for future generations.
